Install this app on your Android phone

Open this page in Chrome on your Android device, then use the browser menu to choose Add to Home screen. This saves the app icon to your home screen for quick launch.

If you are opening the site from another device, use your PC's local IP address instead of 127.0.0.1. For this machine, the working URL is http://10.82.99.164:5000.

Once installed, your HelmetSafe app will open like a normal app with the home-screen icon and clean orange style.
